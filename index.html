Simple sponge cake

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g castor sugar
  • 85 gram self-raising flour

Notes on ingredients

Castor Sugar
Finely gradulated white sugar
self raising flour
A pre-mixed combination of flour and leavening agents(usually salt and baking powder).

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 100 celsius
  2. Grease a 20 cm round cake pan
  3. Whip together the eggs and castor oil
  4. Fold in flour
  5. Pour mixture into the prepared pan
  6. Bake 20 min in the preheated oven
  7. Cool in the pan over a wire rack
